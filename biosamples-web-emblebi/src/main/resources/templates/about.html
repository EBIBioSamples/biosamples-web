About BioSamples

What is BioSamples?

The BioSamples database aggregates sample information for reference samples e.g. Coriell Cell lines and samples for which data exist in one of the EBI's assay databases such as ArrayExpress, the European Nucleotide Archive, or PRIDE. It provides links to assays for specific samples, and accepts direct submissions of samples.

Publications / how to cite

  • Citing BioSample database: please use the following publication: Faulconbridge A., et al., 2014. Updates to BioSamples database at European Bioinformatics Institute. Nucleic Acids Res, 10.1093/nar/gkt1081. Pubmed ID 24265224.
  • Citing BioSample records in your manuscript: please include your sample and group accession numbers and the URL to BioSample home page e.g. "Sample meta-data are available in the BioSamples database (http://www.ebi.ac.uk/biosamples) under accession numbers SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, SAMxxxxxx, and SAMxxxxxx."

The team behind BioSamples

The BioSamples team is part of the Helen Parkinson's team at EMBL-EBI. The team consists of bioinformaticians, ontologists, software engineers and web developers.
